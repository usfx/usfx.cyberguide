How to enjoy gaming securely

Many people love playing games online. It's estimated that 1.2 billion are signing up , logging in and playing online

However since there is alot of things that is money related to gaming such as buying DLC'S or game packages or a game online. criminals can be watching and they might try to take advantage of the situation by stealing your personal data or Credit card details

Here is some steps to help you safeguard your personal information and data when gaming. Whether you are using Console, PC , Phone or tablet. these steps can help you secure your data and personal information online without having the risk to fall for criminals. This steps can leave you free so you can enjoy gaming as much as you can without worrying about your personal information being stolen.

Securing your devices

most of the cyber attacks that happen due to weaknesses in devices or software related. Always keep your software up to date, It will help preent these attacks from being successful.

Keep your system and software updates up to date which is by turning your automatic update on if possible. Always keep in mind to add a layer of defence to your personal device which can be a form of antivirus software. This also needs to be up to date.

Protecting your accounts

Your gaming accounts should be protected with a strong password. A strong password that you don't re-use on any of your other accounts. You should also consider using Two-Factor authentication if available which can also provide you with extra protection to prevent anyone from hacking into your account.

Protecting your privacy

Always keep in mind sharing information online has to be minimum. Apply privacy settings to make sure your personal information is not visible to the other players, and Avoid giving any personal information to other players while playing or in a game.

Using official stores and sources

Whatever console or device you are using to play video gamse. You always should attempt to verify the source or anything that you install. Which is by using official sources and stores

Cyber attacks happens by persuading you as a gamer to do something outside of the game itself. for example, A random player can suggest that you insntall an 'upgrade' and give you a link to download. It also can happen by emails which is by promising some kind of DLC to your game or something extra to the game you are playing which is related to the game you enjoy.

By using offical sources for all your updates and any thing extra you wanna buy for your game. You can avoid malware attacks on your PC, tablet or other devices.
